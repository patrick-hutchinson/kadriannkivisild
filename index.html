Kadri-Ann Kivisild (born in 2000) is an Estonian artist based in Amsterdam. Her practice focuses on art direction, photography and music, drawing inspiration from romance, drama, and peripheral angst.

Building upon her experiences as an Estonian working in Western Europe, Kivisild works with issues related to Western rhetoric and misconceptions about the Soviet Union, modern Russia, and the history of Communism.
